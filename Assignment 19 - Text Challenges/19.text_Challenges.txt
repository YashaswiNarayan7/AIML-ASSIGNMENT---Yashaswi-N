🔹 Preprocessing Needed

To clean the text, the following steps are required:

Convert to lowercase
→ Ensures uniform text
Remove punctuation
→ Removes symbols like !, ?, etc.
Remove emojis
→ Eliminates unnecessary characters
Correct spelling / normalize slang
→ Converts informal words to proper form
Remove stopwords
→ Removes common words like “is”, “the”
Tokenization
→ Splits text into words
Stemming / Lemmatization
→ Converts words to root form
